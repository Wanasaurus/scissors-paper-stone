Let's play scissors, paper, stone!

Pick one of three items: scissors, paper, or stone. The computer will choose a random item in response. Scissors beats paper, paper beats stone, you know how it goes.

For each round, the winner gets one point, while the loser gets zero points. If it's a tie, neither party gets any point.

The first party to attain three points will be the champion.
